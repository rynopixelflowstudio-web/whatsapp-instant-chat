Everything you need to know about the Direct WhatsApp utility.

What is a WA.Link?

A WA.link is a shortened WhatsApp URL (using the official wa.me system) that instantly initiates a chat when clicked. It allows customers or clients to message you without typing your number.

Is my information safe?

Absolutely. This tool runs 100% in your browser. We do not upload, store, or share your phone numbers or messages with any external servers. Completely private.

How do I use the QR code?

Simply generate the link and download the QR code image. You can print it on business cards, flyers, storefront menus, or add it to your website. Anyone who scans it will be linked directly to your chat.
